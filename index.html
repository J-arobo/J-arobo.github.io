About

Postgraduate researcher and Full Stack Developer. I build infrastructure that improves under pressure — six resilience tiers, chaos engineering, LLM-backed adaptive systems running on AWS EC2 with Prometheus, Grafana, Loki, and Locust.

The central finding of my research: a Resilient tier achieves an almost zero HTTP errors under chaos load. That's a measurable antifragility signature, and it's the thesis I'm building.

Beyond research I ship production software — a travel marketplace in Next.js, a food delivery app across Flutter and Laravel. Looking for AI, Full Stack Development, Data or cloud engineering internship roles.
